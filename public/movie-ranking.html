The Best Movies According to Ashish

My top 3 Bollywood movies of all-time.

Dilwale Dulhaniya Le Jayenge

This is my favorite romantic movie. All the songs in this movie are fantastic

Sholay

Really cool movie from late 70s. Brilliant plot and enacted superbly by all the characters

Andaz Apna Apna

Love this comedy movie. Never a dull moment in it.
